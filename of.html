The AMS Mission

The Academy of Medical Sciences at MUST is committed to advancing health in Malawi and beyond through excellence in education, research, and clinical practice. This archive is a cornerstone of our commitment to disseminating knowledge and fostering scientific collaboration.

  • To preserve the scholarly output of our faculty and students.
  • To provide open and equitable access to medical research.
  • To enhance the global visibility and impact of MUST researchers.
  • To support evidence-based healthcare policy and practice.

Key Research Areas

Explore publications across the core research disciplines of the Academy of Medical Sciences.

Infectious Diseases

Leading research on Malaria, HIV/AIDS, Tuberculosis, and other prevalent infectious diseases affecting the region.

Non-Communicable Diseases

Focusing on the growing burden of cardiovascular diseases, diabetes, oncology, and mental health in Malawi.

Public & Global Health

Research in epidemiology, health systems, maternal & child health, and evidence-based policy making.

Surgical & Clinical Sciences

Innovations in surgical techniques, anesthesiology, emergency medicine, and clinical care best practices.

Author & Ethics Policies

We are committed to the highest standards of academic integrity and ethical research conduct.

Peer Review Process

All original research articles undergo a rigorous, double-blind peer-review process to ensure scientific quality and validity.

Plagiarism & Originality

All submissions are screened for originality. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for plagiarism and redundant publications.

Patient Consent & Data Privacy

Research involving human subjects must adhere to strict ethical guidelines, including informed consent and data anonymization.

Copyright & Open Access

Authors retain copyright of their work. We encourage publishing under Creative Commons licenses to promote open access.

Share Your Research

Faculty and postgraduate students of the Academy of Medical Sciences are invited to submit their manuscripts for publication in our peer-reviewed journals.
