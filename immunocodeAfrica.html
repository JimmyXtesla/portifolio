About Us

Bridging the Healthcare Gap Through Innovation

We are a pan-African initiative dedicated to improving healthcare outcomes through locally-adapted digital solutions.

Our mission is to empower healthcare systems across Africa with technology that saves lives

Founded in 2020 by a team of healthcare professionals and technologists, Immunocode Africa addresses critical gaps in healthcare delivery through innovative digital solutions. Our platform is specifically designed for the African context, considering infrastructure challenges and local needs.

We partner with governments, NGOs, and healthcare providers to implement solutions that improve immunization coverage, enhance disease surveillance, and optimize healthcare resource allocation.
