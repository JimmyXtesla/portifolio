About Me

My Economic Perspective

As an Economics student, I am fascinated by the intricate mechanisms that drive markets, influence behavior, and shape public policy. My studies focus on macroeconomics, econometrics, and international trade, equipping me with analytical tools to dissect complex economic issues.

I thrive on translating quantitative data into meaningful insights and exploring the real-world implications of economic theory. I am particularly interested in sustainable development and the role of economic policy in addressing inequality.

Beyond academics, I am an active member of the university's Economics Society and enjoy debating current economic events and policy proposals. My goal is to contribute to research and analysis that fosters economic stability and inclusive growth.
