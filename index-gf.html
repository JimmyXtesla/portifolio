My Journey

I'm a second-year Biomedical Engineering student passionate about merging medicine with technology to create innovative healthcare solutions. My academic journey has been fueled by a desire to understand the human body and develop technologies that can improve lives.

I'm particularly interested in medical device design, biomechanics, and healthcare technology management. My goal is to contribute to developing affordable medical solutions for underserved communities.

"Engineering is not just about solving problems, but about improving lives through innovation and compassion."

When I'm not studying or working on projects, you can find me volunteering at health awareness campaigns, mentoring younger STEM students, or exploring the latest advancements in medical technology.
