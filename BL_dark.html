Go From Idea to Experiment in Minutes

Our platform simplifies the most tedious part of research. We've streamlined the process of protocol creation into three simple steps, transforming complex papers into clear, actionable instructions for your bench work.

  • 1. Upload or Link: Provide a research paper PDF or a link (e.g., PubMed, a journal website).
  • 2. AI Analysis: BioLabGPT's engine parses the text, figures, and tables to extract the complete method.
  • 3. Review & Export: Receive a structured, editable protocol. Refine it, then export it for your lab.
