About the Event

Join us for an inspiring day of exploration and networking at the AMS Career Day, focused on careers in Health and Biomedical Sciences. This event brings together students, researchers, and professionals to share insights, experiences, and opportunities in these dynamic fields.

The program features keynote speeches, panel discussions, poster presentations, and networking sessions with leading institutions and organizations in the health sector.

Don't miss this opportunity to learn from experts, connect with potential mentors, and explore exciting career paths in health and biomedical sciences.
