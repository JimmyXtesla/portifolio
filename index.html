About Me

Driven by curiosity and a passion for impact. Discover the journey.

The Convergence of Code & Cells

I am an aspiring Immunologist and Bioinformatician with a deep-rooted fascination for the intricate dance between the human immune system and the pathogens it encounters. My academic journey at the Malawi University of Science and Technology (MUST) solidified this passion, equipping me with skills in both biological sciences and computational analysis.

My mission is to leverage data science, AI, and robust software development to build practical tools tailored for African health challenges. I focus on deciphering complex biological data (like genomics and flow cytometry) to uncover insights into diseases prevalent on the continent, such as HIV and Tuberculosis.

"Innovation happens at the intersection of disciplines. Let's build impactful solutions together."

Beyond the command line and lab bench, I'm dedicated to fostering the next generation of African innovators through mentorship and actively contribute to open-source projects aiming to democratize scientific tools.
