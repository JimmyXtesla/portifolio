About Me

Get to know the person behind the science and code

The Story

I'm a passionate scientist and developer dedicated to advancing healthcare in Africa through cutting-edge technology. My journey began at the Malawi University of Science and Technology, where I discovered my love for immunology and bioinformatics.

Today, I combine these fields to develop tools that can help researchers better understand immune responses in African populations, particularly in the context of diseases like HIV and TB.

"Let's build the future together and make impact"

When I'm not in the lab or coding, you can find me mentoring young STEM students or working on innovative projects that bridge the gap between biology and technology.
